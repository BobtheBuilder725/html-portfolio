The Best Movies According to Berhan

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Godfather Trilogy

The whole trilogy is amazing, but The Godfather 1, is the greatest movie i have ever seen, the breathtaking cinematography, the complex characters, the rich storyline. This is an easy 10/10

Shawshank Redemption

I love movies about hope, and no movie truly embodies "hope" better than Shawshank Redemption.

Bridge to Terabithia

A Childhood movie that has wonderful cgi for how old it is, great story, nostalgic music and just wholesome.
